# Simple Pong

Files:
- `index.html` — markup with canvas and scoreboard
- `styles.css` — simple styling and layout
- `script.js` — game logic, rendering and input handling

How to run:
1. Put the three files in the same folder.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.

Controls:
- Move left paddle with your mouse (move mouse over the canvas).
- Or use ArrowUp and ArrowDown keys.
- Space to pause/unpause.
- Enter to reset scores and restart.

Notes:
- The right paddle is controlled by a simple AI that follows the ball.
- The ball bounces off paddles and walls; scores update when ball passes either side and the ball resets.
- Ball speed increases slightly on paddle hits up to a max to keep gameplay engaging.

If you want features added (multiplayer, touch support, adaptive difficulty, sound effects, mobile-friendly scaling), tell me which and I'll extend it.
